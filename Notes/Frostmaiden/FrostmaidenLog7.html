20211103 Session Notes

* **Karla** attacks the dragon with *"Shadowbolt"* and only does a little damage.

* **Conor** yells "Grab the dead!"

* Begin with **Orikeo** confused about Shar masquerading as Selune and intercepting the communication between us and Selune. He talks to **Sig**, his father, about this revelation and questions their worship to a false diety.

* **Sig** tries to use *"Divine Intervention"* but with the recent revelation it fails due to Selune being his diety of choice.

* **Sig** then says the name of **Conjurer Demouranth** and one of the eight (8) doors open.

* Looking in the opened door, he sees a vortex of glowing stars, rotating in the middle of the room and eight (8) high back chairs, each with one of the symbols for the schools of magic.

* We also notice constellations on the ceiling and a large black star in the middle, where no star exists in today's sky.

* Upon looking at the black star, a few of us become afraid to look at the night sky because the Ebon star might be looking back.

* Next we introduced our characters for the streaming audience.

* **Tyche** then mentally told us she wants to "Get the fuck out!"

* After some searching, we find books on the ground around the high back chairs. **Tyche** begins to read one and finds out the door to **Ithorus's** chamber only opens if three (3) or more of his apprentices sit in the high-back chairs.

* We also find and **Farrok** identifies a *"Scroll of Commet"*, which is a level 11 or 12 legendary spell from before **Mystra**.

* As a group we discuss how and when to strategically use the scrolls to summon a Tarrasque and a Commet as a boss CIA move.

* **Sig** looks around more while we are discussing the scrolls.

* **Tyche** decides now is a good time for some tricks, and magically pulls a panther out of her bag!

* **Conor** looks closer at a crack in the wall that seemed to drain the magical essence from magical items placed near by.

* The group hides around a corner and watches as **Farrok** places a hand on the crack. The crack was powerful enough to extract a whole spell slot from him!

* **Roscoe** shoots a *"Firebolt"* at the crack and the bolt is completely consumed.

* He keeps shooting and each and every bolt is consumed by the crack.

* **Conor** lock picks a double door, it opens to a balcony which overlooks the city.

* **Farrok**, **Orikeo**, and **Tyche** sit in the three chairs with symbols that match the cantrips they received in another building earlier, and a portal opens up to **Ithorus's** chamber.

* **Sig** is the first through, **Farrok** is pushed through by **Orikeo** when he asks to hold hands to go through the portal.

* We see a chamber that hasn't been disturbed for centuries. There is a large gap and three (3) one-eyed creatures on the other side.

* Looking closer we see a floating skull with runes forming above it. It's a demi-lich!

* ***ROLL INITIATIVE!***

* **Conor** goes first and hypnotizes the Gnothics with a pattern. And inspires **Orikeo** in a way only a Bard can.

* Demi-lich gives us all lair damage! And then makes us all take a Con save.

* **Sig** drops to the ground, then immediately starts breathing again because his name is in **Tyche's** book.

* **Conor** also drops to the ground then is surrounded by the Gnothics.

* Another Con save for everyone.

* **Tyche** uses darkness to hide and fly up.

* **Orikeo** moves up to grab **Conor** and get him safely away from the Gnothics. In the process, **Orikeo** draws attacks from the Gnothics.

* **Farrok** moves up and uses a chain-force-lighting to hit all the Gnothics and the Demi-lich.

* The Gnothics are obliterated from **Farroks's** attack!

* **Roscoe** next via voice chat, summons Kujo next to the Demi-lich and he takes fire damage. Then he heals **Conor**.

* **Sig** then heals himself.

* The Demi-lich uses a legendary action and now Kujo is blinded!

* A gold orb moves towards **Orikeo** and a golden rectangle tries to consume the space where he is standing.

* **Oyea** moves up as far as she can before the Demi-lich moves towards her and tries to suck her life force.

* Next **Orikeo** is cursed!

* **Tyche** says the liches name and then uses a spell from Fizban.

* **Roscoe** and Kujo then take turns attacking the Demi-lich with as much fire damage as they can.

* **Orikeo** seeing how good fire damage is, uses his inner-druid to produce flame, hit the Demi-lich, then move into the darkness before getting rid of the curse.

* **Farrok** uses *"Bigby's Hands"* to hold and try to crush the Demi-lich.

* **Sig** calls forth his spiritual weapon which is a goblin with an old timey news hat and newspaper which says "Liches Suck!" in Draconic.

* The golden orb that was near **Orikeo** moves towards **Farrok** and successfully swallows him into a Demi plane with two (2) flesh golems and three (3) magen.

* They all ***ROLL INITIATIVE!*** against **Farrok**, he immediately shields as much of their attacks as he can.

* **Conor** attacks the demi plane as the Demi-lich moves into the darkness cast by **Tyche**.

* **Sig** then finds himself surrounded by am anti-magic field.

* **Farrok** takes lightning damage in the Demi plane as **Tyche** calls forth a fog cloud while dropping darkness.

* At the same time the Demi-lich calls a dust cloud while **Roscoe** hits him with a *"Scorching Ray"*. Then Kujo does more fire damage.

* **Orikeo** attacks with thunder damage.

* **Farrok** turns invisible and hides in the demi plane until we can get him out.

* **Sig** feels around for the skull and calls his spiritual weapon towards him to damage the Demi lich.

* Flesh golems attack **Farrok** and miss every attack.

* **Roscoe** avoids getting swallowed by the demi plane.

* **Conor** shouts "Turn off the fog!"

* **Tyche** does and **Conor** casts a spell at the Demi plane, it pops and **Farrok** and his friends all come out to greet us.

* **Conor** then casts unsettling words on the Demi lich.

* Demi lich howls and we all take damage.

* Field drops on **Sig**, he is magical again.

* **Tyche** falls and takes a death save.

* **Roscoe** and Kujo do more fire damage to the demi lich.

* **Orikeo** uses his last charm of invisibility to move and heal **Tyche**.

* **Tyche** then goes down again.

* **Farrok** kills the Demi lich with a firebolt!

* **Roscoe** makes the skull able to heal us.

* **Farrok** takes advantage of it, then **Sig** heals us all a lot!

* **Conor** hypnotizes the enemies left.

* **Tyche** mind lances the golem.

* Then we all finish off the last enemies.

* We search the room

* Find the word to deactivate the shield, Saladganar

* Staff of poser!

* Spell book of Ithorus!
