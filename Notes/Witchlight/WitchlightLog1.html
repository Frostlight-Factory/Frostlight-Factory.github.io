20211117 Session Notes

** Katinka, Bellasar, and Vellia arrive at the carnival individually, and recognize each other while waiting in line to enter.

** Zayln walks up to the line, Kaylen notices he's missing an eye, without hesitation points it out and ask him about it.

** Upon reaching the ticket booth, the goblin attendant greets us, and hands us a box with tickets already purchased by an anonymous benefactor.

** However, to enter the carnival we have to don and continue to wear a pair of appropriately sized butterfly wings, which are also handed to us.

** As soon as she receives and puts on her wings, Vellia runs up to a monkey playing a calliope and starts to dance around with him.

** Katinka tries to convince the monkey to join us, however he's too busy, you know, doing his job.

** After wandering away from the monkey, a squirrel passing out dandilions comes up to us and hands us each one and tells us to blow it and make a wish! (A secret we each tell the DM)

** Next we come to the "high point" of the carnival, a BUbble Teapot Ride! It costs one ticket punch and we get to ride in bubbles and see the whole carnival from the air! They are tough to steer though.

** Since only a few of our party can control the bubbles, we end up in different parts of the carnival.

** Zayln, Bellasar, and Vellia all end up at the carousel of unicorns. Zayln and Vellia start talking to Diana, who is a Centaur working on the carousel.

** Katinka and Kaylen eventually make their way to us at the carousel.

** We find the door leading into the carousel's center, and find four riddles to solve. They appear to be names of unicorns on the carousel.

Fortune, B _ _ _ _

Fall, P _ _ _ _ _ _

Stone, M _ _ _ _ _

Stitch, N _ _ _ _

{Bold, Pride, Moss, Nine}

** After we solve the riddle, the unicorns on the carousel start to move, and we get a free ride for fixing it!

** As we are enjoying the ride, the unicorns talk to us and inform each of us who has what we are each missing, and the benefactors of the carnival; The Hourglass Coven of Hags!

** We are each told which hag controls our curse, and what they are each allergic to.

** Barlona Blightstraw is allergic to seeing someone in "Widdershins."

** And we get a descriptions of the lair of the hags from the unicorns.

** We look over at Harvey, and he is grabbing randomly at the air. Apparently he is seeing "invisible floating fish" after smoking some grass with a carnie.

** Katinka pulls out and straps something on her arm.

** We all hear a weird, unsettling whisper, seemingly coming from Zayln, but his lips aren't moving.

** Kaylen pokes at the weird eyeball tattoo on Zayln's chest, it looks evil.

** Zayln then asks to see our weapons and see what kind of attacks we know.

** Harvey pulls out his rapier and engages in a little friendly sparring.

** Next Bellasar pulls out his scimitar and also engages in a little sparring.

** When Vellia is asked to show her weapon, she hold up an innocent looking staff and says "I'm not much of a fighter," then Zayln finds himself knocked on his ass mysteriously.

** Kaylen looks scared after Vellia laughs at the position in which Zayln finds himself. Then Kaylen too, finds himself knocked on his ass!

** Zayln screams at a group of kids to scare them. However he does a horrible performance and the kids laugh at him instead.

** Kaylen changes into something else, {pic on discord}, and we all make a wisdom save.

** End Session 1
