20220316 Session Notes

** Additions to last session:

** We got a creepy vibe off Jingle Jangle.

** The Harengons were singing about Agnar Longcock, he was not actually with them.

** We are heading towards Downfall via the Briggands Tollway.

** Quinn starts talking to Zaylen about him getting sick all of a sudden, and the creepy eye in his chest is starring directly at Quinn.

** Zaylen kept talking about a great leviathan, and some other random shit.

** We all unanimously agree.

** While traveling, we come to a small camp for one (1). And in the ashes of the fire pit we find a partially burned book.

** From what's left of it, we decipher the author is looking for a shot at Agnar and to find a shipment of guns, which shouldn't be here in the Feywild.

** We arrive at the tollway, and it's full of plywood structures, in shit shape, above the high tide.

** About 300 ft away, where the plywood structures converge, we see a very large tree stump.

** To get through, we come up with a plan to sell the snails we acquired to the Briggands.

** While discussing the plan, a mysterious hooded figure joins us on the wooden walkway.

** We each hear a whisper in our head Agdon Longscarf.

** As we continue down the walkway towards the stump, we see two (2) skiffs full of Harengon appear out of the fog!

** He asks for anyone with a firearm to hand it over, and for us to help him find and burn the cache of weapons the briggands stole. His purpose here is to get rid of all firearms and ammunition.
