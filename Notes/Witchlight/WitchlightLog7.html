20220323 Session Notes

** Starting in the Taxidermy Room of Bavlorna's cabin, Vellia finds a fox pelt and puts it on like a cape. Bo'Az finds a Basilisk corpse in a cage and figures out how to get some of the venom out of it's skin.

** Vellia also finds a wooden eyeball, and keeps it.

** Harvey helps Bo'Az by delicately cutting out the venom sack from the Basilisk.

** Zaylen starting talking about his stealth numbers. And Harvey asked "Do you measure your stealth in numbers?!"

** Weaver put up his hood, and became more difficult to see, then started to move out of the Taxidermy room to see what was ahead.

** While searching the next room with a pool and a well, Vellia casts "Unseen Servant" to send it to the well.

** Meanwhile Zaylen walks right up to the well, looks in, and moves away.

** Weaver is looking at a mirror on the wall, says "Bandersnatch," and a portal back to the hall of mirrors in the Carnival opens up.

** Vellia then sends her unseen servant to the well to reach in, the spell is destroyed as soon as it touches something in the well!

** Harvey casts "Mage Hand" and it too gets destroyed trying to grab something in the well.

** Bellesar opens up a door to a balcony full of frogs. He goes to grab one, and they all start a loud "CROAK!" in unison.

** Harvey keeps seeing shadows move out of the corners of his eyes, and they resemble "Shadow Children" that he has heard about before.

** While Bo'Az is opening another door to a room with a cupboard, Bellesar throws the little, innocent, defenseless frog in to the well. It dies a painful, agonizing death screaming in absolute pain. What a monster!

** Harvey opens the sigil on the cupboard and finds some stuff inside. A rolled up piece of paper that says "Spittlespew."

** Bo'Az looks in a mirror, and his face appears expressionless. Everything else is normal in the mirror, just faces have no expression.

** Bo'Az then opens the last door, and it's a fabric room.

** We discuss heading towards the direction that Harvey saw the shadow move, and Quinn leads the way.

** Continuing through the cabin, Zaylen comes to a room with a boiler and a bunch of pipes connected to it. It looks like some kind of magical still. Of course he disturbs it, and someone opens a door on the other side of the room and stares directly at Zaylen.

** The potion master, as we come to find out, introduces himself as Crow.

** We talk for a min or two, then he asks to see the book we brought to return to Bavlorna.

** While Crow and Vellia are talking about the book, Harvey uses "Mage Hand" to remove the fox pelt off of her.

** We talk more to Crow and ask if we can help free him from the Hags service and control.

** He informs us that the frogs on the balcony were an alarm, a collective "OOOHHHH" was heard, and Bavlorna already knows we're here.

** Crow knows about Kaylen and Ragnar, and where they are located.

** Crow then talked about ambushing Bavlorna to kill her, as that's the only way he'll be free.

** He also informs us that "Widdershins" is a dance, and he reluctantly shows us how to do it.

** He also tells us about the ritual in progress to turn our once companions in to Hags.

** While preparing for the fight with Bavlorna, he mixes some of his blood with the Basilisk venom to make a "Potion of Hag Slaying" for some of our weapons.

** Walking through the kitchen on the way to Bavlorna's chambers, we help the butcher,Bloody Shoes, catch the vultures he's chasing. Crow then tells him to take the rest of the day off and go somewhere else.

** Reaching Bavlorna's room, Zaylen's stuffed crab crawls up Bavlorna's shoulder and starts talking to him!

** As soon as that happens, he wants to start a fight to get his crab back. Even though it has been awakened by Bavlorna.

** ROLL INIATIVE!

** Bavlorna's Quicklings act first and attack Harvey six (6) times! He feels the pain!

** Zaylen then "Hexes" Bavlorna and puts a "Shatter" to hit her and a Darkling Eldar.

** Next Bo'Az shoots a quickened "Magic Missile" and kills one Quickling before shooting his crossbow at another but missing.

** Bavlorna looks at Vellia, says "You look tasty..." then she hops over and swallows Vellia whole!

** Vellia immediately falls unconscious in Bavlorna's belly from the acid damage.

** Harvey attacks another Quickling before inspiring Zaylen.

** Bellesar casts "Toll the Dead" on Bavlorna and crits on the damage!

** Getting into the fight now, Crow casts "Scorching Ray" and hits Bavlorna.

** Using the power of the Rune Knight, Quinn grows in size before reaching in Bavlorna's mouth to try and rescue Vellia.

** The acid damage hurts, but isn't enough to keep him from pulling Vellia out before she gets dissolved inside Bavlorna.

** Trying to jump in and help Bavlorna, the Darkling Eldar casts "Darkness" that covers everyone in the room except Zaylen and Crow.

** Zaylen attacks the Darkling Eldar with his spear and adds a "Branding Smite".

** Bo'Az quickly heals Vellia as much as he can before moving away from Bavlorna.

** Bavlorna then spits out two (2) more Quicklings, and Quinn gets six (6) attacks from the surrounding Quicklings.

** Vellia "Misty Steps"away to get out of harms way while she heals more.

** Harvey then inspires Bellesar before hopping away to safety himself.

** Bellesar attacks Bavlorna with his "Spiritual Weapon" followed by Crow shooting a few "Magic Missiles" at her.

** Quinn then starts punching the Quicklings.

** Zaylen moves up to attack Bavlorna with his trident before trying to bite her.

** Bo'Az "Magic Missiles" Bavlorna before she "Polymorphs"herself into a Great Ape.

** The Quicklings attack Bo'Az.

** Vellia calls forth a "Phantasmal Force" in the form of a Poacher pointing a gun directly at the Great Ape.

** Harvey moves up to stab the Great Ape before Bellesar calls "Toll the Dead" on it.

** Crow then causes the "Polymorph" to drop on Bavlorna with a "Scorching Ray" attack.

** Quinn Death Save.

** Zaylen misses a trident attack before biting Bavlorna. It hurts, but she likes it!

** Bo'Az then shoots more "Magic Missiles" at her.

** Bavlorna turns to attack Zaylen but misses.

** The Quicklings continue attacking Bo'Az.

** Harvey calls "Cure Wounds" on Quinn before inspiring Bellesar.

** Bellesar casts "Toll the Dead" again before attacking her with his "Spiritual Weapon".

** Crow tries a spell attack, but fails.

** Quinn stands up, and stabs Bavlorna with a dagger.

** Zaylen hits her with a "Branding Smite" to the side of the face.

** Bo'Az uses some sorcery to be able to cast one last "Magic Missile", and it works! Bavlorna is dead!

** As soon as she is killed, the remaining Quicklings disappear.

** DING! LEVEL UP! {5}

** Bellesar finds an Iron Key on Bavlorna.

** Crow casts "Cure Wounds" on Kaylen before opening up the potion cabinet Vellia found. Inside are three (3) potions, one to shrink, one to grow, and one to vanish.

** Kaylen and Ragnar get up, we help them find their gear and items and get suited up. We talk about what happened the night they disappeared, the Darkling Eldar is what grabbed them with claws from the bridge.

** Mr. Crabs talks to Zaylen, but doesn't want to be his companion anymore. lol

** We make it to Bavlorna's bed room and find a locked chest. Crow opens it with a "Knock" and inside are three (3) spell scrolls of "Identify" and a signed, un-punched carnival ticket.

** We make it to another room, both Iron Keys the group has opens the door, we see a large frog statue in the corner, and we find a Helmet of Telepathy, which Harvey claims.

** Bo'Az sitcks his hand in the frogs mouth, and pulls it out with no ill effects.

** Vellia sticks her hand inside, and pulls out a little stick, 2" long, and it feels very magical. She then gets help to pull the magic out of the stick and gains her missing height back!

** Bo'Az then tells Kaylen he can probably get his humanity back if he sticks his hand in the frog's mouth. Kaylen sticks his hand in, and pulls it out with his middle finger flicking off Bo'Az.

** Learning about the mirror out of the Feywild, and back into the carnival, Quinn, and Ragnar decide they are leaving as soon as they can, and ask Bo'Az to go with them.

** Bo'Az declines and stays with his new friends.

** DING! LEVEL UP! {6}

** END SESSION!
